Extension Installation Guide
Download Extension

In the chrome browser click on the three dots in the top right corner.

Go down to More Tools and then click on Extensions.

Enable developer mode by clicking on the slider in the top right corner.

Extract the zip folder you got from clicking the Download Extension button.

Drag and drop the extracted extension folder into the chrome extension page.

Confirm that the FLARE extension is enabled by making sure that the slider is blue and to the right on the extension page.

If You Run Into Any Problems:

In the top right corner click on the FLARE extension icon.

Turn off the "enable cross-origin" slider, and turn it back on.

While holding shift, press the F5 key to do a hard refresh.
